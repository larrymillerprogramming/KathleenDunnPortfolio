Kathleen Dunn is a Boston-based choral musician with a passion for sharing her art with students of all ages. She is creative in her instruction and uses her knowledge of vocal pedagogy to help students achieve artistic success while maintaining healthy vocal production. Kathleen believes in a student-centered approach to music making and learning, allowing singers to take ownership of their artistry.
